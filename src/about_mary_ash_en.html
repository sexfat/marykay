Founder of Mary Kay, a Pioneer of the Times!

“Sometimes we feel incapable enough and doubt whether we can make our own part to change the world. Trust me! You Can Do It!”
-- Mary Kay Ash

Mary Kay Ash, an outstanding leader who started her own business more than 50 years ago with US$ 5,000 savings and built a “dream company” subverting the business cultures and working environment dominated by males, thereby creating more opportunities for women in the workplace.

Her unswerving belief and golden rules have inspired millions of women. Mary Kay didn’t believe that there is so-called iron lady but “everyone is the dark horse as long as he/she holds on”. By doing so, all your dreams will come true!

Mary Kay Ash’s most proudest legacy is that there are nearly 40 countries around the world so far committed to empowering women to create their own businesses, thus helping thousands of women all over the world to sparkle and shine.

Mary Kay Origin

After 25 years of hard work in the direct selling industry, Mary Kay resolutely decided to retire. She wants to build a "dream company" and become a force to support women. Provide opportunities for women to have a deeper imagination and hope for themselves and life and increase the index of happiness and positiveness.
In 1963, she founded MARY KAY with a deposit of US$5,000 and recruited 9 beauty consultants to start selling products and promoting business. In the first year of the establishment of Mary Kay, the number of Mary Kay beauty consultants has grown from 9 to 300, with an annual performance of nearly 200,000 US dollars!
By the 1980s, Mary Kay’s part-time beauty consultants had an annual income of about 4,000 US dollars, and the average annual salary of 4,000 business supervisors was 60,000 US dollars. Mary Kay is very proud to say that in her company, women earn 50,000 US dollars a year. The number of people ranks first in the United States!
In 1969, the pink car award program was established and became a trophy symbolizing beauty consultant honor. This is the pink power of Mary Kay, soft but strong. Through color, we can not only beautify our external appearance, but also bring energy, change our lives.
